CLINICASENSE AI - DEMO CLINICAL KNOWLEDGE BASE

TOPIC: CHEST PAIN AND DYSPNEA

This document is a demonstration knowledge resource for
the ClinicaSense AI prototype.

Chest pain accompanied by shortness of breath may represent
a potentially serious clinical presentation. Clinical
assessment should consider the patient's symptoms, medical
history, vital signs, physical examination, and appropriate
diagnostic evaluation.

Important clinical considerations may include:

- Assessment of the severity and characteristics of chest pain.
- Evaluation of vital signs and oxygen saturation.
- Review of cardiovascular risk factors.
- Consideration of an electrocardiogram when clinically appropriate.
- Assessment for signs of acute coronary syndrome or other
  potentially serious causes.
- Consideration of pulmonary and other differential causes
  based on the clinical presentation.

Patients with severe, worsening, or concerning symptoms may
require urgent medical evaluation.

This document is intended only as a demonstration knowledge
source for an AI clinical decision support prototype.

It does not provide patient-specific diagnosis or treatment
recommendations.
