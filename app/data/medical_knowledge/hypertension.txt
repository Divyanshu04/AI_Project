CLINICASENSE AI - DEMO CLINICAL KNOWLEDGE BASE

TOPIC: HYPERTENSION

This document is a demonstration knowledge resource
for the ClinicaSense AI prototype.

Hypertension is a condition characterized by persistently
elevated blood pressure. Clinical evaluation should consider
blood pressure measurements obtained using appropriate
technique and repeated measurements when clinically
appropriate.

Important clinical considerations may include:

- Review of blood pressure measurements and measurement technique.
- Assessment of cardiovascular risk factors.
- Review of medical history and relevant comorbidities.
- Review of current medications and adherence.
- Assessment of lifestyle factors such as dietary patterns,
  physical activity, smoking, and alcohol use.
- Consideration of renal function and other relevant laboratory
  evaluation based on the clinical context.
- Assessment for symptoms or findings that may suggest
  hypertensive emergency or acute target-organ involvement.

Severely elevated blood pressure accompanied by concerning
symptoms or evidence of acute organ injury requires urgent
clinical assessment.

Management decisions should be individualized by qualified
healthcare professionals based on the complete clinical picture.

This document is intended only as a demonstration knowledge
