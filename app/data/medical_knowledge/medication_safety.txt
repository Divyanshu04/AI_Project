CLINICASENSE AI - DEMO CLINICAL KNOWLEDGE BASE

TOPIC: MEDICATION SAFETY

This document is a demonstration knowledge resource
for the ClinicaSense AI prototype.

Medication safety assessment should consider the complete
medication list, allergies, medical history, kidney and liver
function, and potential interactions.

Important clinical considerations may include:

- Review of all prescribed and non-prescription medications.
- Review of medication allergies and previous adverse reactions.
- Assessment of possible drug-drug interactions.
- Assessment of possible drug-disease interactions.
- Review of medication dosing and adherence.
- Consideration of renal and hepatic function when relevant.
- Review of recently started or discontinued medications.
- Assessment of symptoms that may represent an adverse
  drug reaction.

Patients experiencing severe allergic reactions, difficulty
breathing, severe swelling, significant bleeding, loss of
consciousness, or other potentially serious adverse reactions
may require urgent medical evaluation.

Medication changes should be reviewed with an appropriately
qualified healthcare professional.

This document is intended only as a demonstration knowledge
source for an AI clinical decision support prototype.

It does not provide patient-specific diagnosis or treatment
recommendations.
