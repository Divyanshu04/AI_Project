CLINICASENSE AI - DEMO CLINICAL KNOWLEDGE BASE

TOPIC: RESPIRATORY CONDITIONS

This document is a demonstration knowledge resource
for the ClinicaSense AI prototype.

Evaluation of respiratory symptoms should consider the
duration, severity, associated symptoms, medical history,
and physical findings.

Important clinical considerations may include:

- Assessment of respiratory rate and oxygen saturation.
- Evaluation of shortness of breath and its severity.
- Assessment for cough, fever, chest discomfort, or wheezing.
- Review of smoking and environmental exposure history.
- Consideration of asthma, infection, pulmonary embolism,
  cardiac causes, and other differential diagnoses when
  clinically appropriate.
- Review of relevant past respiratory and cardiovascular history.
- Consideration of appropriate diagnostic evaluation based
  on the clinical presentation.

Severe difficulty breathing, low oxygen saturation, cyanosis,
altered mental status, or rapidly worsening respiratory symptoms
may require urgent medical evaluation.

Clinical assessment and management should be performed by
qualified healthcare professionals.

This document is intended only as a demonstration knowledge
source for an AI clinical decision support prototype.

It does not provide patient-specific diagnosis or treatment
recommendations.
