CLINICASENSE AI - DEMO CLINICAL KNOWLEDGE BASE

TOPIC: DIABETES

This document is a demonstration knowledge resource
for the ClinicaSense AI prototype.

Clinical evaluation of a patient with possible or known diabetes
should consider symptoms, medical history, laboratory findings,
medications, and relevant risk factors.

Important clinical considerations may include:

- Review of symptoms such as increased thirst and urination.
- Review of blood glucose measurements when available.
- Consideration of HbA1c testing when clinically appropriate.
- Assessment of cardiovascular risk factors.
- Review of renal function.
- Assessment of medication use and adherence.
- Evaluation of lifestyle factors and nutritional patterns.
- Consideration of complications associated with prolonged
  hyperglycemia.

Patients with symptoms suggesting severe hyperglycemia,
significant dehydration, altered mental status, or other
concerning findings may require urgent clinical evaluation.

Clinical management should be individualized by qualified
healthcare professionals.

This document is intended only as a demonstration knowledge
source for an AI clinical decision support prototype.

It does not provide patient-specific diagnosis or treatment
